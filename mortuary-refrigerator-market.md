<p><p>The mortuary refrigerator market is primarily categorized into temporary storage refrigerators and body freezers. Temporary storage refrigerators are designed for short-term preservation of bodies awaiting identification or funeral arrangements, maintaining an appropriate temperature to delay decomposition. Body freezers, on the other hand, provide long-term storage solutions, offering lower temperature environments to preserve bodies for extended periods, often utilized in medical facilities or research institutions. Both types are crucial for ensuring the respectful handling of remains while maintaining hygiene standards.</p></p>
