<p><ul><li>Piezo Buzzers</li><li>Magnetic Buzzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The buzzer market is primarily divided into two types: piezo buzzers and magnetic buzzers. Piezo buzzers operate using piezoelectric materials, producing sound through mechanical vibrations, making them compact and energy-efficient. They are widely used in consumer electronics, alarms, and toys. Magnetic buzzers, on the other hand, use electromagnetism to generate sound, often resulting in louder output. These are typically found in applications requiring higher sound levels, such as automotive and industrial devices. Both types serve diverse applications across various industries.</p></p>
