<p><p>The coaxial cable market comprises two primary types: baseband and broadband coaxial cables. Baseband coaxial cables transmit digital signals over short distances, commonly used in local area networks (LANs) for data transmission. Conversely, broadband coaxial cables are designed for high-frequency applications, supporting multiple channels for video, internet, and voice communications. They are widely used in cable television and internet services. Both types play crucial roles in data communication, ensuring efficient and reliable signal transmission across various applications.</p></p>
