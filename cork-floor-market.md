<p><strong>The Cork Floor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pasting Cork Floor</li><li>Lock Cork Floor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cork floor market is primarily divided into two types: pasting cork floors and lock cork floors. Pasting cork floors involve adhesive application to secure the tiles or planks directly to the subfloor, providing a seamless and stable surface. In contrast, lock cork floors feature interlocking mechanisms that allow for easier installation, as they do not require adhesive. Both types offer excellent insulation, durability, and eco-friendliness, catering to various consumer preferences and installation needs in residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
