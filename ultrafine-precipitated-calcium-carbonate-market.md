<p><p>The ultrafine precipitated calcium carbonate (PCC) market is segmented into uncoated and coated types. Uncoated PCC is typically used in applications requiring high brightness and purity without additional surface treatment, making it suitable for industries like paper and plastics. In contrast, coated PCC is treated with surface modifiers to enhance its performance characteristics, such as hydrophobicity and dispersibility.
