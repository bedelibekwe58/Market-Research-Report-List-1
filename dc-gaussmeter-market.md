<p><p>The DC Gaussmeter market is categorized into two main types: 1-axis DC Gaussmeters and 3-axis DC Gaussmeters. A 1-axis DC Gaussmeter measures magnetic fields along a single axis, making it suitable for applications requiring directional sensitivity in one dimension. In contrast, a 3-axis DC Gaussmeter can measure magnetic fields simultaneously across three orthogonal axes, providing comprehensive data on field strength and direction. These devices are essential in various industries, including electronics, materials testing, and research environments.</p></p>
