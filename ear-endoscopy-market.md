<p><strong>The Ear Endoscopy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Middle Ear Endoscopy</li><li>Inner Ear Endoscopy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ear endoscopy market is segmented into two primary types: middle ear endoscopy and inner ear endoscopy. Middle ear endoscopy focuses on examining and treating conditions affecting the middle ear, such as otitis media or cholesteatoma. It employs minimally invasive techniques to visualize the eardrum and surrounding structures. Inner ear endoscopy, on the other hand, targets deeper structures like the cochlea and vestibular system, facilitating diagnosis and treatment of complex disorders such as hearing loss or balance issues.</p></p>
