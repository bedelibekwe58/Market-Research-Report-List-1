X-ray equipment enables internal examination by producing images using ionizing radiation, critical for identifying fractures and tumors. Infrared devices detect thermal radiation to monitor body temperature and identify inflammation or infection. Mass spectrometers analyze the chemical composition of biological samples, providing insights into diseases at a molecular level. Together, these technologies enhance patient care and diagnostics.</p></p>
