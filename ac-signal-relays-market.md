<p><p>The AC signal relays market is segmented into TH (through-hole) mounting and SMT (surface-mount technology) mounting types. TH mounting involves inserting pins through holes in a printed circuit board, providing strong mechanical support and ease of maintenance, making it suitable for high-power applications. In contrast, SMT mounting allows for smaller, more compact designs, suitable for high-density circuits, enhancing manufacturing efficiency. Both types cater to diverse applications in industrial, automotive, and consumer electronics, influencing growth patterns in the relay market.</p></p>
