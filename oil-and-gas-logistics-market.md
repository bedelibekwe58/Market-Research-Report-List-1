<p><p>The oil and gas logistics market is categorized into three key segments: upstream, midstream, and downstream. Upstream logistics focus on exploration and production, involving the transportation of drilling equipment and raw materials to extraction sites. Midstream logistics deal with the transportation, storage, and distribution of crude oil and natural gas, utilizing pipelines and tankers. Downstream logistics involve refining crude oil into products like gasoline and petrochemicals, as well as the distribution of these finished products to consumers and retailers.</p></p>
