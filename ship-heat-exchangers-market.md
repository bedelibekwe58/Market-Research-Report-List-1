<p><ul><li>Shell and Tube Heat Exchangers</li><li>Plate Heat Exchangers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ship heat exchangers market primarily features two types: shell and tube heat exchangers and plate heat exchangers. Shell and tube heat exchangers consist of a series of tubes enclosed in a shell, allowing fluid exchange between the tubes and shell, suitable for high-pressure applications. Plate heat exchangers, on the other hand, consist of multiple plates that create thin flow channels, offering higher heat transfer efficiency in a compact design. Both types are crucial for effective thermal management in marine systems.</p></p>
