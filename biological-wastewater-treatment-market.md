<p><strong>The Biological Wastewater Treatment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aerobic</li><li>Anaerobic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Biological wastewater treatment markets are primarily divided into aerobic and anaerobic processes. Aerobic treatment utilizes microorganisms in the presence of oxygen to break down organic pollutants, effectively reducing biochemical oxygen demand (BOD) and producing stable byproducts. In contrast, anaerobic treatment operates without oxygen, relying on specific bacteria to decompose organic matter, generating biogas that can be harnessed for energy. Both methods are crucial for sustainable wastewater management, each offering distinct advantages depending on the nature of the wastewater and desired outcomes.</p></p>
