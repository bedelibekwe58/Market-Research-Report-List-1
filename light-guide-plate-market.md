<p><p>The Light Guide Plate (LGP) market features several types based on design and manufacturing techniques. Edge LGP utilizes light from the edges for uniformity, while Bottom LGP directs light from a back source. Printed LGP involves patterns for enhanced light diffusion, contrasting with Non-printed LGP which maintains a simpler design. Injection Molding LGP is produced using molding techniques for complex shapes, while Cut Forming LGP involves cutting processes for custom designs, catering to diverse applications in display and lighting industries.</p></p>
