<p><strong>The PET/MRI System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fully Integrated</li><li>Separate Devices</li><li>Pulse Oximetry Screening</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PET/MRI system market comprises three main types: fully integrated systems, separate devices, and pulse oximetry screening technologies. Fully integrated systems combine positron emission tomography (PET) and magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) into a single unit, providing simultaneous imaging for enhanced diagnostics. Separate devices consist of independent PET and MRI machines that can be used in conjunction for comprehensive assessment. Pulse oximetry screening, while not directly related to PET/MRI, supports patient monitoring by measuring oxygen saturation, highlighting the importance of multi-modal imaging approaches.</p></p>
