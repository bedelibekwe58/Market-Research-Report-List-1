<p><strong>The Cross-Linked Polyethylene Pipes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Peroxide Cross-Linked Pipe</li><li>Silane Cross-Linked Pipe</li><li>Irradiation Cross-Linked Pipe</li><li>Azo Cross-Linked Pipe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cross-Linked Polyethylene (PEX) pipes are categorized into four main types based on their cross-linking methods. Peroxide cross-linked pipes employ high-temperature processes with peroxide to enhance durability and resistance. Silane cross-linked pipes utilize moisture to initiate cross-linking, resulting in flexible and robust pipes suitable for plumbing. Irradiation cross-linked pipes involve exposure to electron beams, creating a highly resilient structure. Azo cross-linked pipes utilize azo compounds for cross-linking, offering unique thermal and chemical properties, enhancing pipeline applications across various industries.</p></p>
