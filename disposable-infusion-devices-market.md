<p><p>The disposable infusion devices market comprises two primary types: pump infusion sets and non-pump infusion sets. Pump infusion sets provide precise control and delivery of medications via programmable infusion pumps, offering enhanced accuracy for patient care. Conversely, non-pump infusion sets facilitate gravity-fed administration of fluids and medications without mechanical assistance, making them simpler and cost-effective alternatives. Both types are essential in various medical settings, catering to diverse patient needs while ensuring safety and hygiene through disposable components.</p></p>
