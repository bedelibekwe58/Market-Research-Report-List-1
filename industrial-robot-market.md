<p><ul><li>Articulated Robots</li><li>Parallel Robots</li><li>SCARA Robots</li><li>Cylindrical Robots</li><li>Cartesian Robots</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial robot market encompasses various types, each serving specific applications. Articulated robots feature rotating joints, providing flexibility ideal for complex tasks. Parallel robots consist of arms connected to a fixed base, offering high precision and speed, suitable for assembly. SCARA robots, with a horizontal arm configuration, excel in pick-and-place operations. Cylindrical robots utilize a cylindrical coordinate system, ideal for assembly and packaging. Cartesian robots operate on a linear three-axis system, favoring straightforward tasks like material handling and 3D printing.</p></p>
