<p><p>The industrial sewing needle market comprises several types tailored for specific stitching techniques. Lockstitch sewing needles are designed for standard fabric seams, ensuring strong and consistent stitches. Interlock sewing needles are ideal for knit fabrics, allowing elasticity and stretch. Overlock sewing needles are used in overlock machines for finishing edges, preventing fraying while enabling stretch. Chainstitch needles create durable stitches often used in seams and decorative applications. Each type plays a crucial role in different garment and fabric production processes.</p></p>
