<p><p>The Micro Heat Pipe market includes various types, each designed for efficient thermal management. Pulsating Heat Pipes utilize a working fluid that oscillates, enhancing heat transfer in compact spaces. Microgrooved Plate Heat Pipes feature micro-sized grooves that maximize surface area for improved heat conduction. Loop Heat Pipes circulate a working fluid in a closed loop, effectively transferring heat over larger distances with minimal temperature variation. Together, these technologies cater to applications in electronics, aerospace, and advanced cooling systems.</p></p>
