<p><p>Terahertz and mid-infrared spectroscopy markets are categorized based on wavelength ranges: near-infrared (NIR), mid-infrared (MIR), and far-infrared (FIR). NIR typically covers 0.75 to 2.5 micrometers, ideal for analyzing organic compounds and materials. MIR spans 2.5 to 25 micrometers, widely used for molecular identification and chemical analysis. FIR, ranging from 25 to 1000 micrometers, offers insights into low-frequency vibrations and rotational transitions of molecules, enhancing applications in materials science and biomedicine. Each type provides unique insights for diverse analytical needs.</p></p>
