<p><strong>The Hyperspectral Imaging Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spatial Scanning Technology</li><li>Spectral Scanning Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hyperspectral imaging technology encompasses two primary market types: spatial scanning technology and spectral scanning technology. Spatial scanning technology captures images by sequentially scanning a scene, acquiring spectral data at different spatial locations. It excels in applications requiring detailed spatial resolution. In contrast, spectral scanning technology analyzes specific wavelengths across a wide spectral range, focusing on the spectral characteristics of materials. This method is advantageous for in-depth material identification and analysis, making it suitable for fields such as agriculture, environmental monitoring, and defense.</p></p>
