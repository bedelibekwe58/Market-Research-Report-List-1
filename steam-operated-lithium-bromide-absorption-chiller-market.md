The double effect chiller is more efficient, utilizing steam to drive two cycles of absorption, resulting in higher cooling output and lower energy costs. Conversely, the single effect chiller operates with one absorption cycle, offering a simpler design and lower initial investment, ideal for smaller cooling applications.</p></p>
