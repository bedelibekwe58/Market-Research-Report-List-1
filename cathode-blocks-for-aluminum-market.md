<p><p>Cathode blocks are essential components in aluminum smelting, used in electrolytic cells to conduct electricity. The market for cathode blocks can be categorized into three types: semi-graphitic, graphitic, and graphitized. Semi-graphitic blocks contain a blend of carbon and graphite, offering moderate conductivity and thermal properties. Graphitic blocks utilize high-purity graphite, delivering superior conductivity and longevity. Graphitized blocks are subjected to high-temperature treatments, enhancing their thermal stability and performance. These variations cater to different operational needs in aluminum production.</p></p>
