<p><strong>The Plastic Strapping Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PP Strapping</li><li>PET Strapping</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic strapping market is primarily divided into two types: polypropylene (PP) strapping and polyethylene terephthalate (PET) strapping. PP strapping is lightweight, flexible, and often used for lighter loads and indoor applications due to its cost-effectiveness. In contrast, PET strapping is stronger, more durable, and suitable for heavy-duty applications, making it ideal for packaging and securing heavier items in various industries. Both types are widely used for bundling, palletizing, and providing stability during transportation.</p></p>
