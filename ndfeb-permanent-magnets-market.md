<p><strong>The NdFeB Permanent Magnets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bonded Ndfeb</li><li>Sintered Ndfeb</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The NdFeB permanent magnets market can be primarily divided into two types: bonded NdFeB and sintered NdFeB. Bonded NdFeB magnets are produced by combining magnetic powder with a binder, allowing for complex shapes and improved isotropy, making them suitable for applications like small motors and sensors. In contrast, sintered NdFeB magnets are made from compacted and sintered magnetic powder, offering higher magnetic strength and are typically used in applications requiring superior performance, such as electric vehicles and wind turbines.</p></p>
