<p><strong>The Wafer Pre Aligner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vacuum Aligner</li><li>Atmosphere Aligner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wafer pre-aligner market is categorized into two main types: vacuum aligners and atmosphere aligners. Vacuum aligners utilize suction to hold and position wafers accurately during the alignment process, ensuring high precision and stability. In contrast, atmosphere aligners operate in standard environmental conditions, relying on mechanical means for alignment. Both types are essential in semiconductor manufacturing, with vacuum aligners typically preferred for their superior accuracy, while atmosphere aligners offer a cost-effective solution for less critical applications.</p></p>
