<p><p>The industrial cleaning system market comprises two primary types: wet cleaning and dry cleaning. Wet cleaning utilizes water and specialized detergents to remove contaminants from surfaces, making it effective for removing heavy soil and grime. This method is often used in industries where hygiene is critical, such as food processing. Dry cleaning, on the other hand, employs solvents instead of water to clean delicate materials or areas sensitive to moisture. It is ideal for maintaining equipment and surfaces in sectors like manufacturing and electronics.</p></p>
