<p><p>Radiation shielding windows are essential for protecting against harmful radiation in various environments, such as medical facilities and nuclear plants. Conversely, leaded acrylic windows offer a lightweight and shatter-resistant alternative, suitable for areas where safety and visibility are paramount. Both types serve to create safe environments, with leaded glass being more traditional and leaded acrylic increasingly favored for its versatility and reduced weight.</p></p>
