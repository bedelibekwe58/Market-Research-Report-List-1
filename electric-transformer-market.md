<p><strong>The Electric Transformer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isolation Transformers</li><li>Autotransformers</li><li>Toroidal Transformers</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric transformer market consists of various types, each serving distinct applications. Isolation transformers provide electrical isolation between circuits, enhancing safety and reducing noise. Autotransformers, utilizing a single winding, are more compact and efficient for voltage regulation. Toroidal transformers, characterized by their doughnut shape, offer low electromagnetic interference and are space-efficient. Additionally, the "Other" category encompasses specialty transformers, including phase-shifting and step-up/step-down transformers, tailored for specific industry needs and applications. Each type contributes uniquely to diverse electrical systems.</p></p>
