<p><p>The Mobile Clean Room market is segmented into Soft Wall and Hard Wall types. Soft Wall clean rooms utilize flexible fabric materials to create removable barriers, offering adaptability and convenience for temporary setups. They are often used for short-term projects and ease of transport. In contrast, Hard Wall clean rooms are constructed with rigid panels, ensuring maximum structural integrity and contamination control, making them suitable for permanent installations in industries requiring stringent environmental standards. Both types cater to specific operational needs.</p></p>
