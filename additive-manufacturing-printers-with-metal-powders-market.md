<p><p>Additive manufacturing with metal powders encompasses different printing technologies, primarily including Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electron Beam Melting (EBM). SLM utilizes a high-powered laser to selectively melt and fuse metal powders layer by layer, allowing for intricate designs and strong parts. EBM employs an electron beam in a vacuum to achieve similar results, typically at higher build rates and lower residual stresses. Other technologies in this market may include binder jetting or direct energy deposition, expanding applications in various industries.</p></p>
