<p><ul><li>Intensity Autocorrelators</li><li>Interferometric Autocorrelators</li><li>Scanning Autocorrelators</li><li>Single-shot Autocorrelators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The autocorrelators market includes various types tailored for different applications. Intensity autocorrelators measure signal strength variations over time, while interferometric autocorrelators utilize phase shifts in light to analyze pulse width and integrity. Scanning autocorrelators employ mechanical movement to capture temporal profiles, making them effective for fine measurements. Single-shot autocorrelators, on the other hand, capture pulses in one go, ideal for high-speed events. This diversity caters to fields like telecommunications, scientific research, and optical engineering.</p></p>
