<p><p>The external sound card market is primarily divided into two types: Bluetooth and USB. Bluetooth sound cards offer wireless connectivity, allowing users to connect to devices like smartphones and laptops without cables, providing convenience and mobility. USB sound cards, on the other hand, connect directly to a computer's USB port, typically offering superior sound quality and lower latency, making them ideal for professional audio work and gaming. Both types cater to varied user preferences, enhancing audio experiences across devices.</p></p>
