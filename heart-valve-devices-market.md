<p><strong>The Heart Valve Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heart Valve Replacement Devices</li><li>Heart Valve Repair Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heart valve devices market comprises two primary types: heart valve replacement devices and heart valve repair devices. Heart valve replacement devices involve surgically replacing damaged valves with prosthetic ones, either mechanical or biological, to restore normal blood flow. In contrast, heart valve repair devices aim to restore the function of existing valves through techniques such as annuloplasty or edge-to-edge repair, often less invasive. Both types are crucial for treating heart valve diseases and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
