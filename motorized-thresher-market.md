<p><p>The motorized thresher market is categorized into several types based on their mechanisms. The spike-tooth type employs spikes to separate grains from the straw, ensuring efficient threshing. The axial flow type uses a cylinder design to facilitate continuous grain movement, enhancing throughput. The hammer mill type utilizes high-speed hammers to pulverize and separate crops, while the wire-loop type employs a wire mechanism for gentle threshing. Other types may include specialized designs tailored for specific crops or farming practices.</p></p>
