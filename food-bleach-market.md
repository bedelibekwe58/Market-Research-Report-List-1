<p><p>The food bleach market is categorized into two main types: reduced type and oxidized type. Reduced type food bleaches, such as sodium bisulfite, primarily serve to lighten and stabilize color in products without affecting flavor. In contrast, oxidized type bleaches, like benzoyl peroxide, promote color alteration and enhance shelf life by modifying the chemical structure of food components. Both types are essential in food processing, improving appearance and quality while ensuring compliance with safety standards.</p></p>
