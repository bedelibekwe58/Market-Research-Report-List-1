<p><p>Single screw pumps are positive displacement pumps that utilize a single helical screw rotor to move fluids. This design allows for the effective handling of a wide range of viscosity levels and is suitable for transferring various types of fluids, including those that are shear-sensitive. Primarily used in industries such as oil and gas, food and beverage, and wastewater treatment, single screw pumps offer advantages like low pulsation, simple construction, and ease of maintenance.</p><p>The Single Screw Pumps Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.6% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for efficient fluid transfer systems across various applications.
