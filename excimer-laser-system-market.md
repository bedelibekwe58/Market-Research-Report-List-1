<p><p>The Excimer Laser System market is categorized into three primary types: </p><p>1. **Slit Scanning Laser System**: Uses a narrow slit to scan the eye, providing continuous treatment for larger areas and enhancing precision.</p><p>2. **Spot Scanning Laser System**: Employs a focused beam to treat specific areas, allowing for detailed correction and customization in laser surgeries.</p><p>3. **Wave-Front Scanning Laser System**: Measures and corrects higher-order aberrations based on individual eye mapping, offering personalized treatment for improved visual outcomes.</p></p>
