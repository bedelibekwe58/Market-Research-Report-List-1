<p><strong>The Smart Plugs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bluetooth-Type</li><li>Wi-Fi-Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart plugs market can be segmented into two main types: Bluetooth-type and Wi-Fi-type. Bluetooth-type smart plugs communicate using Bluetooth technology, offering a straightforward, low-power option ideal for short-range connections. They are often easier to set up and use but have limited range. Conversely, Wi-Fi-type smart plugs utilize home Wi-Fi networks for connectivity, enabling remote control from anywhere with internet access. This type typically supports more features, such as voice control and integration with smart home ecosystems, making them popular for advanced smart home setups.</p></p>
