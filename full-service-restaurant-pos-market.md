<p><p>The Full Service Restaurant POS market can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise systems. Cloud-based POS solutions operate over the internet, allowing for remote access, automatic updates, and lower upfront costs. They often provide flexibility and scalability, catering to restaurants of varying sizes. In contrast, on-premise POS systems are installed directly on-site, offering greater control and security. However, they typically require higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance, making them suitable for establishments with specific infrastructure needs.</p></p>
