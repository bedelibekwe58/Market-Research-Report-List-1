<p><strong>The Residential Fuel Cell Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PEM</li><li>SOFC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential fuel cell market is primarily categorized into two types: Proton Exchange Membrane (PEM) and Solid Oxide Fuel Cells (SOFC). PEM fuel cells operate at lower temperatures and provide quick start-up times, making them suitable for residential applications with immediate energy needs. In contrast, SOFCs operate at higher temperatures and offer higher efficiency and fuel flexibility, ideal for continuous power generation. Both technologies contribute to clean energy solutions, enhancing energy independence and reducing carbon footprints in residential settings.</p></p>
