<p><p>The 3D intraoral scanners market is primarily divided into white light and laser technologies. White light scanners utilize LED-based illumination to capture dental impressions through optical triangulation, providing quick and accurate digital models. In contrast, laser scanners employ laser beams to obtain high-precision 3D images, offering superior detail and depth perception. Both types enhance diagnostic capabilities and streamline dental procedures, but their application may vary based on specific clinical needs and practitioner preferences.</p></p>
