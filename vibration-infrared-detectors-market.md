<p><p>The vibration infrared detectors market is divided into two primary types: Passive Cooling (PC) and Active Cooling (PV) detectors. PC detectors rely on materials that naturally cool to detect infrared radiation without external power, making them energy-efficient and suitable for various applications. In contrast, PV detectors employ active cooling systems, enhancing sensitivity and performance, particularly in challenging environments. Both types cater to diverse industry needs, such as security, automotive, and environmental monitoring, each offering distinct advantages in temperature management and detection precision.</p></p>
