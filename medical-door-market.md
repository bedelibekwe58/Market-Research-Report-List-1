<p><p>The medical door market includes three primary types: airtight doors, radiation protection doors, and magnetic screen doors. Airtight doors are designed to maintain environmental control in sterile hospital environments, preventing contamination. Radiation protection doors shield patients and staff from harmful radiation in areas such as radiology and nuclear medicine. Magnetic screen doors provide non-contact barriers that allow for easy passage while controlling airflow and preventing pest intrusion, contributing to a hygienic atmosphere in medical facilities.</p></p>
